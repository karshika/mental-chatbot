Mind Relaxing Games

Take a break to calm your mind and refocus with our collection of simple, relaxing games.

Pop floating bubbles to relieve stress. Each pop is a release of tension. Click on them before they float away.

A guided breathing exercise to calm your nervous system. Inhale for 4s, hold for 7s, exhale for 8s.

Zen Doodle

Doodle freely on a blank canvas. Let your mind wander as you draw simple shapes and colors.

Connect the Stars

Connect stars to form a beautiful constellation. A slow, meditative focus exercise.

Grow a Plant

Click repeatedly to nurture and grow a small digital plant. Watch it bloom with each click.
